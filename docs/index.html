Ship's Log

A diary by Capt. JT 'Skully' Bones

Thar be monsters in the sea

The Mighty Kraken

Every sailor's greatest fear. While there's only one of the Mighty Kraken, it can roam anywhere in the sea in utter silence. There is no recourse or planning to deal with the Kraken aside from hoping and praying you don't cross its path.

The Slithering Depths

A faceless terror. Akin to the Kraken, the Slithering Depths move stealthily and strike mercilessly. The creature can swallow up the largest vessels with only its extremities, suggesting a monstrosity of ungodly size lurks beneath the surface.

Sirens of the Darkness

The Sirens of the Darkness lure in ocean voyagers with angelic singing as a mirage stretched across the horizon. However, steer clear of the chorus, lest you get too close and they reveal their true form, as a horror of the depths!

By JT Bowen
