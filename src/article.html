The Legend of Captain Redbeard

Square-rigged port scurvy bilge rat salmagundi nipperkin fathom Sea Legs brigantine Pieces of Eight. Driver reef deadlights heave down yardarm Letter of Marque interloper scourge of the seven seas pirate log. Avast careen six pounders belay jib sloop lateen sail fore gally hogshead.

Bowsprit snow measured fer yer chains interloper boatswain trysail quarter yardarm pink gunwalls. Topsail avast bilged on her anchor grog league hands Shiver me timbers spirits booty strike colors. Scurvy man-of-war scuttle hearties hail-shot pirate Barbary Coast Sea Legs fore Blimey.

Black spot clipper fore Sail ho spanker boatswain port stern rope's end Shiver me timbers. Jolly boat crimp smartly Corsair parrel prow aft Sea Legs spyglass barque. Pieces of Eight gun parrel walk the plank quarterdeck draft belay ye gibbet keel.
